Kuzana Referral Program Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: July 25, 2024

  1. Eligibility: This program is open to anyone who refers a business that meets Kuzana's investment criteria.
  2. Referral Submission: Referrals must be submitted through the official Kuzana referral link generated on kuzana.co/referral/. The referred business must apply using this unique link for the referral to be valid.
  3. Investment Criteria: Kuzana invests $10k-$100k in Kenyan SMEs (agri-processing, real estate/construction, manufacturing, fintech, retail, logistics) with Ksh 300k - 10m monthly revenue. The business should generally be 3 months to 5 years old. Full criteria are available on kuzana.co.
  4. Referral Bonus: A bonus of up to Ksh 50,000 will be paid to the referrer if Kuzana successfully invests in the referred company. The exact amount depends on the size of the investment and will be communicated upon successful investment closure.
  5. Payment: The referral bonus will be paid via M-Pesa or bank transfer within 30 days after the investment funds are disbursed to the referred company.
  6. Exclusions: Self-referrals are not eligible. Referrals of businesses already in active discussion with Kuzana may not be eligible.
  7. Modification/Termination: Kuzana reserves the right to modify or terminate this referral program at any time without prior notice.
  8. Disputes: Any disputes regarding eligibility or bonus payments will be resolved at Kuzana's sole discretion.
